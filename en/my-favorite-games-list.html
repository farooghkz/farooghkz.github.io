My favorite games list

WIP: this page is under development yet

This is a list of good games which I have in my Ubuntu box and I would like to introduce them to other.

Terminal/Console games

  • moon-buggy: In this game you drive a machine on moon's surface and jump over craters.
  • pacman4console : As its name says, it's a simple pacman game with characters, there aren't a lot of levels there(I even couldn't win these levels...) but comes with a level editor.
  • nethack: I didn't play its console version, but the graphical version was good so I guess its console version would be cool, too.
  • bsdgames: It's not a game but a collection of nice games like worm, hunt, snake, etc as well as utilities like pig, primes, rot13, etc.
  • ninvaders: a console version of the famous game Space Invaders.
  • 2048.c If you have played the famous game 2048, it's just a nice console version of it. if not: with smaller tiles you should make bigger ones(2 and 2 will be 4, 4 and 4 =8 and so on), you will win when you reach the 2048 tile. with a little knowledge of C, or even without it, you may make modifications to the game and for example use a 5x5 grade instead of the default 4x4.

Graphical games which doesn't require much powerful PCs

  • LiquidWar: You are a liquid and you should turn other liquids into your color(or eat them), you are win when you are the only liquid in the filed. It is multiplayer and also it supports networking.
  • Atanks or Atomic tanks is a multi player turn based stratagy game which you should destory other tanks while protecting your own, you can buy new weapons, shields, etc with the money you earn from damaging other tanks. Also it supports networking
